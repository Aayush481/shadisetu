About Sadhi Setu

Sadhi Setu is a modern wedding platform that connects wedding families with trusted vendors. From photographers, decorators, and caterers to mehndi artists, musicians, and venues – everything you need for a wedding can be explored and booked on one platform.

Our vision is to make wedding planning stress-free, transparent, and memorable. We bring families and vendors together on a reliable system where communication is simple and trust is the foundation.
